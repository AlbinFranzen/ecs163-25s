Pokémon Dashboard

In this dashboard for ECS163 we explore Pokémon attributes. A Pokémon has the attributes health, attack, defense, special attack, special defense and speed. They also have a primary and secondary type. For my animations I used a drill-down approach with brushing to select cateogries in the parallel coordinates plot, selection for the stacked bar chart and an animation for the ridgeline plot.
